New Locations:

Alaverdi Monastery, which was founded in the 6th century by Joseph Alaverdel , one of the 12 Assyrian Fathers . It is located in the Alazni Valley , in the background of the snowy mountains of the Caucasus Range, 20 km from Telavi . Today's St. George's temple was built in the first quarter of the 11th century by King Kvirike Kakhta on the site of an old small church. One of the largest cathedrals in Georgia (41.7 m X 26.4 m) is more than 50 meters high.

Narikhala (Narin Kala) is an ancient fortress overlooking Tbilisi and the Mtkvari River . Narikala ( Persian: Narinkala - citadel, Shidatsikhe), is the late name of Tbilisi Castle - Kala. It was mentioned in the sources for the first time in 1772 with the German traveler Johann Guldenstedt . The castle consists of two sections of the wall, which is located on a steep rise between the sulfur baths and the botanical garden . In the lower courtyard of Narikala, newly restored St. Nicholas Church and a small cafe are located there.

Okatse Canyon is a natural monument in the vicinity of Zeda Gordi village of Khoni municipality .The canyon is located in the Okatse river valley. The length of the river is 14 kilometers, and the width of the canyon is 3-6 meters in some places, 15-20 meters in others, and the depth varies from 20 to 100 meters. The canyon is decorated with several waterfalls. One of the lakes, Oskhapo, is 60 meters long. There are several natural stone bridges on the Okatse River , one of them is "Boga". The canyon has caves from which karst waters flow.

Prometheus Cave , Tskaltubo Cave , Kumistavi Cave - karst cave in Georgia, 6 km northeast of Tskaltubo , in the territories of Kvilishor and Kumistavi , Tskaltubo municipality . It is located on the Tskaltubo massif , 0.5 km to the northeast of Vaucluse of Kumi. It is a subhorizontal, corridor-type cavity. It has three entrances: one is located at the bottom of a dry valley at an altitude of 147 m above sea level , the 2nd opens at 160 m, and the 3rd opens from the Vaucluse of Qum through the chain of siphonic lakes at 125 m. It is also possible to enter the cave from the cave of Ghliana, through a 200 m watery Talan.

The Lopot is a river in the far north-eastern part of the Kakheti region in Georgia. It flows through the village Lapanquri, and discharges into the Alazani near Telavi. Its upper reach borders with the Russian North Caucasian Republic of Dagestan and was scene of a Georgian anti-insurgency operation in August 2012.

The history of the castle starts from the 9th century . Its original name was "Lomsia", which was destroyed during the wars of the 11th century. At the beginning of the 12th century, the rebuilding of the city was started by Bashken II Jackel, who, under the instructions of Davit Agmashenebeli, created fortification defense systems against the Seljuks and rebuilt the cities. It was during this period that this place was named "Akhaltsikhe". In the 12th - 13th centuries, the castle became the residence of jackals .
